About Me

Hello, my name is Sam. I'm the kind of person who likes to learn something new every single day of my life. Commitment motivates me, and I do it consistently. I study at La Verdad Christian College as a BSIS major and a sophomore. I'm a member of the Members Church of God International.

What really got me interested in programming was basically starting with computers and wanting to understand how they work. It reached a point where I wanted to make my own games since I play games too. There's something amazing about creating something that others can enjoy and interact with.

When it comes to technology, everything new excites me it's just so cool. Like there's always something incredible happening.

I'll be honest, I'm a slow learner, but that's just another fact about me. I've discovered that taking my time actually helps me understand things more deeply. It might take me longer to grasp certain concepts, but when I finally get it, the knowledge really sticks with me.
